Fitness Center

Our state-of-the-art fitness center is designed to help you maintain your fitness routine during your stay. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting out, our fitness center offers a variety of options to suit your needs.

Group Exercise Classes

We offer a range of group exercise classes led by certified instructors. Our classes include yoga, Pilates, Zumba, spinning, and more. Check our schedule for class times and availability.

Personal Training

Our certified personal trainers are available to help you create a customized workout plan and provide guidance on proper exercise techniques. Personal training sessions can be booked at the front desk.
